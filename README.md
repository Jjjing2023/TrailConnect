## Play Store Description
TrailConnect is the ultimate social platform for hiking enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned mountaineer or just starting your outdoor journey, TrailConnect makes it easy to discover new trails, connect with fellow hikers, and share your adventures.

Explore detailed trail information including distance, elevation, difficulty, maps, and recent conditions. Join group hikes hosted by verified users, outdoor influencers, or gear brands. Share photos, post trip reports, and join trail-specific chat groups to plan your next adventure.

Our app makes outdoor exploration more social and rewarding. You can find and connect with hikers nearby, track your progress, and earn badges and rewards for visiting new locations. TrailConnect encourages safe, sustainable exploration and fosters real-life community among nature lovers.

> **Slogan:**  
> *Connect! Hike! Belong!*

## 🎯 Target Users
Young adults (ages 20–35) who enjoy hiking — solo or with others — and are looking for a way to:
- Discover new trails
- Find hiking groups
- Document their outdoor experiences socially

## 📝 Common Themes from Competitor Reviews
- Desire for more social features (e.g., sharing, connecting with others)
- Frustration with outdated or inaccurate trail info
- Motivation through gamification, badges, or tracking
- Lack of verified group hikes and difficulty finding communities

---

## 🔍 Key Focus Areas
1. **UI:** Modern, intuitive design with map overlays and media-rich trail cards
2. **Shared Data:** Real-time user-generated content and trail updates
3. **Messaging:** In-app chat groups for events, nearby hikers, and shared interests
4. **User Engagement:** Badges, rewards, and progress tracking to boost retention

---

## 🧩 Libraries and APIs
- **National Park Service API:** Official park data, events, alerts, and more
- **Firebase:** Real-time messaging, authentication, cloud data storage
- **Glide / Picasso:** Efficient image loading and caching
- **Location API:** Nearby trails and hikers discovery
